Who am i?

Welcome to my whimsical world of scrunchies! I'm Sweta Gupta, the girl with big dreams and an even bigger love for all things scrunchie. Picture me with a twinkle in my eye and a scrunchie-adorned ponytail bouncing as I invite you into my colorful realm of hair accessories. Oh, let me tell you, starting this business was quite the adventure! I faced more hurdles than a bunny in a field of carrots, but you know what? I jumped right over them with a skip in my step and a determination that could move mountains (or at least stacks of scrunchies!). From the very beginning, I encountered challenges that could have made me shy away, but I brushed off the doubts like loose strands of hair and dove headfirst into my scrunchie-filled dreams.
